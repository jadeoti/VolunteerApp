Who we are

Edu-Aid is a Non-Governmental Organization of Covenant Christian Centre established with the intent of facilitating access to qualitative public primary education in Nigeria. We do this through a number of programmes and initiatives in selected public primary schools around Lagos State. We are currently based in 10 schools in Lagos and our PROGRAMMES includes:

  • Literacy and Mentoring Programme (LAMP)
  • Arts & Design Programme
  • Leadership Book-Club Programme
  • Numeracy, Engineering, Science and Technology (NEST)
  • Teacher Conferences
